Frequently Asked Questions

How much does fence installation cost in Tampa?
Fence installation in Tampa costs $1,800–$13,800 for a typical residential yard. Most homeowners spend $5,400 for 150–200 linear feet of standard wood privacy fence.
What's the cheapest fence option in Tampa?
Chain-link is the most affordable fence type in Tampa at $14–$20 per linear foot installed. Split-rail wood is the next cheapest at $16–$23/ft.
How long does fence installation take in Tampa?
Most residential fence projects in Tampa take 1–3 days. Larger projects (300+ ft) or installations requiring permits can take 3–5 days plus permit wait time.
Do I need a permit for a fence in Tampa?
Most cities including Tampa require a permit for fences over 6ft, or near property lines. Budget 1–3 weeks for permit approval. Check with Tampa building department before installation.
