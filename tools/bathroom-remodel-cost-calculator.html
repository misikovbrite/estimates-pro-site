Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this calculator?

This calculator uses 2026 average US contractor rates and adjusts for your region. Estimates are typically within 15–20% of actual contractor quotes. For a precise quote, we recommend getting estimates from 2–3 local contractors.

What's not included in this estimate?

This estimate doesn't include permit fees ($200–$1,500), structural changes, mold remediation, or unexpected issues found during demolition. Add 10–15% as a contingency buffer.

How long does a bathroom remodel take?

A typical full bathroom remodel takes 3–4 weeks. A simple cosmetic update (fixtures, paint) can be done in 2–3 days. Complex remodels with plumbing or tile work can take 4–6 weeks.
